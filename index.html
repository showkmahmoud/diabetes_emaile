Understanding Well-Being:

Well-being consists of physical, mental, and emotional health 3 Key factors include balanced nutrition, regular exercise, stress management 4

Why Well-Being Matters:

Millions of people with diabetes face daily challenges managing their condition. Diabetes care often focuses only on blood sugar, leaving many overwhelmed. By promoting well-being, we can help those with diabetes lead healthier, more fulfilling lives.
